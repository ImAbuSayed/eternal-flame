[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
I feel the embers burning in the dark
A quiet fire starting from a spark
Your fingertips are pulling me so near
There is no place I want to be but here

[Verse 1]
The shadows dance so slow across the wall
I listen closely as the midnight calls
A subtle glance that takes my breath away
I read the words you never have to say

[Pre-Chorus]
The hour fades, the quiet starts to hum
I feel the rhythm of a beating drum
Just pull me close and let the world rewind
Leave every single heavy thought behind

[Chorus]
We built an eternal flame tonight
A steady glow that makes the shadows right
I want to lose myself inside your eyes
And watch the morning color up the skies

[Post-Chorus]
Oh, let it burn, let it pull us in
This is the moment where we both begin

[Verse 2]
The scent of pine and ashes in the air
I trace the gentle falling of your hair
A whispered secret resting on my skin
I open up and let the magic in

[Pre-Chorus 2]

[Post-Chorus 2]

[Bridge]

A quiet fire starting from a spark

My open spirit follows where you lead
